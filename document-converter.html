  • PDF: Ideal for sharing documents while preserving layout

Conversion Tips

  • PDF to DOCX: Best for editing PDF content in Word
  • DOCX to PDF: Ideal for sharing finalized documents
  • TXT to DOCX: Convert plain text to formatted documents
  • For scanned documents, OCR may be needed for text extraction

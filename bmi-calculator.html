Understanding Your BMI

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple index of weight-for-height that is commonly used to classify underweight, normal weight, overweight, and obesity in adults.

  • BMI is not a direct measure of body fat but correlates with body fat measurements
  • It may not be accurate for athletes, pregnant women, or the elderly
  • Consult a healthcare provider for a comprehensive health assessment

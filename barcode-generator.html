About Barcode Types

Code 128

High-density alphanumeric barcode supporting all 128 ASCII characters. Widely used in shipping and packaging industries.

Format: Alphanumeric
Length: Variable

EAN-13 & UPC-A

Standard barcodes for retail products worldwide. EAN-13 is used internationally, UPC-A is primarily used in North America.

EAN-13: 13 digits
UPC-A: 12 digits

Industrial Barcodes

CODE39: Alphanumeric, used in automotive and government. ITF-14: Used for cartons and outer cases. Pharmacode: Pharmaceutical tracking.

CODE39: Alphanumeric
ITF-14: 14 digits

About Unit Conversions

Length Conversions

Convert between meters, kilometers, centimeters, millimeters, miles, yards, feet, and inches. 1 meter = 3.28084 feet.

Weight Conversions

Temperature Conversions

Convert between Celsius, Fahrenheit, and Kelvin. Fahrenheit = (Celsius × 9/5) + 32. Kelvin = Celsius + 273.15.

Volume Conversions

URL encoding converts characters into a format that can be transmitted over the internet. Decoding reverses this process.

How to Use the URL Encoder/Decoder

  • To encode: Enter or paste your text in the input field and click "Encode URL". Special characters will be converted to percent-encoded format.
  • To decode: Enter or paste a URL-encoded string in the input field and click "Decode URL". Percent-encoded characters will be converted back to readable text.
  • Use the "Clear" button to reset both input and output fields.
  • Use the "Copy Output" button to copy the result to your clipboard.

Example: Encoding "Hello World!" produces "Hello%20World%21". Decoding "https%3A%2F%2Fexample.com%2Fpage%3Fid%3D1" produces "https://example.com/page?id=1".

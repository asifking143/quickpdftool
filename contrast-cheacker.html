About WCAG Contrast Ratios

  • WCAG AA (Normal Text): Requires a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1 for normal text.
  • WCAG AAA (Enhanced): Requires a contrast ratio of at least 7:1 for normal text.
  • Large Text: Text that is at least 18pt (24px) or 14pt (18.5px) and bold requires a ratio of at least 3:1 for AA.
  • How to use: Select foreground and background colors using the color pickers, enter color codes, or click preset colors.
  • Color formats supported: HEX (#RRGGBB), RGB (rgb(r,g,b)), and HSL (hsl(h,s%,l%)).

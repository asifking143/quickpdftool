Understanding GST

GST Rates in India

India has four primary GST slabs: 5% (essential goods), 12% (processed foods), 18% (standard rate for most goods and services), and 28% (luxury and sin goods).

CGST vs SGST

GST is divided into CGST (Central GST) and SGST (State GST). For intra-state transactions, GST is split equally between center and state (e.g., 9% CGST + 9% SGST = 18% GST).

Exclusive vs Inclusive

GST Exclusive: Price shown does not include GST. GST Inclusive: Price shown already includes GST. This calculator handles both scenarios.

GST Invoice

A valid GST invoice must show the GST amount separately, along with GSTIN of both supplier and recipient, HSN/SAC code, and place of supply.

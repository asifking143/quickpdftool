Privacy Policy for QuickPDFTool

Last Updated: [Date]

1. Introduction

Welcome to QuickPDFTool. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our PDF processing tools.

QuickPDFTool is a client-side HTML/JavaScript application that processes your PDF files locally in your browser. We do not have servers that store or process your PDF files.

2. Information We Collect

2.1. PDF Files and Content

All PDF processing occurs locally in your web browser. We do not:

  • Upload your PDF files to our servers
  • Store your PDF files on any server
  • Access the content of your PDF files
  • Transmit your PDF files over the internet

2.2. Technical Information

We may collect minimal technical information for functionality and analytics:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Device type
  • Anonymous usage statistics (features used, errors encountered)
  • Local storage preferences for your convenience

2.3. Cookies and Local Storage

We use:

  • Local Storage: To save your preferences and tool settings locally on your device
  • Session Cookies: To maintain your session and application state
  • Analytics Cookies: Anonymous usage data to improve our tools

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain the PDF processing functionality
  • To improve user experience and tool performance
  • To fix bugs and errors
  • To understand which features are most useful to our users
  • To ensure the security and proper functioning of our tools

4. Data Security

Since all PDF processing happens locally in your browser:

  • Your PDF files never leave your computer unless you choose to download or save them
  • We implement industry-standard security practices in our code
  • We regularly update our tools to address security vulnerabilities

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:

  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Hosting: Static file hosting (e.g., GitHub Pages, Netlify)
  • CDN: Content delivery networks for library files

These services may collect anonymous usage data as per their own privacy policies.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Use our tools without providing personal information
  • Clear your browser's local storage and cookies
  • Disable JavaScript (though this will break functionality)
  • Use browser extensions to block analytics
  • Not use our tools if you disagree with this policy

7. Children's Privacy

Our tools are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. The "Last Updated" date at the top will reflect changes. We encourage you to review this policy regularly.

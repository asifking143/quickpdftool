Password Security Tips

Strong Passwords

Use at least 12 characters with a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols. Avoid dictionary words and personal information.

Password Entropy

Entropy measures password randomness in bits. Higher entropy means stronger passwords. Aim for at least 60 bits for important accounts.

Regular Updates

Change passwords regularly, especially for sensitive accounts. Never reuse passwords across different websites or services.

Password Managers

Consider using a password manager to securely store and generate passwords. This allows you to use unique, strong passwords everywhere.

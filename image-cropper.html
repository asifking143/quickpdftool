How to Crop

  1. Upload an image using the area above
  2. Click and drag on the image to select crop area
  3. Adjust selection using the handles at corners
  4. Click "Crop Image" to apply the crop
  5. Download your cropped image

Cropping Tips

  • Hold SHIFT while dragging to maintain aspect ratio

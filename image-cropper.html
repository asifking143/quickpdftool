How to Use the Image Crop Tool

  1. Click the "Choose Image" button or drag and drop an image into the upload area.
  2. Use the zoom and rotate controls to adjust your image as needed.
  3. Click and drag on the image to select the area you want to keep.
  4. Click "Apply Crop" to see the cropped preview.
  5. Click "Download Image" to save your cropped image to your device.
